# Approximating Subset-Performance Distribution in Active Learning under Sample Constraints

This repository contains the research proposal for my Bachelor's Thesis at Goethe University Frankfurt (MLO lab).

## 📄 Abstract / Overview
Current active learning (AL) query methods often offer limited improvements over random sampling in pratice . In this thesis, AL serves as the motivation, while the primary research objective is the approximation of subset-performance distributions under limited samples. In this way, the prevalence of high-performance subsets can be quantified by approximating the distribution.
